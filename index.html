Our Heart of it All: F.I.R.S.T.T. Generation

F.I.R.S.T.T. Generation is our non-profit, dedicated to feeding and inspiring seniors 65+. We provide vital care, love, and support through teamwork. From bill pay assistance and house cleaning to lawn services and fresh homemade meals, we’re committed to helping those who need it most.

During the holidays, we deliver hot meals to homebound seniors, ensuring they never feel forgotten. A percentage of our profits goes toward this noble cause, and we invite you to join our efforts in making a difference in our community.

About Us

Family Story

It's all been and survives because of the Lord and from love...

Chef Carrie boasts an illustrious 20-year fine dining experience, serving diners of all backgrounds and palates. With experience as a dietitian, she effortlessly prepares meals of diverse culinary origins. Carrie’s creations, including her famous homemade chili, specialty green beans, spinach dip, and her dynamic carving meat station, regularly receive rave reviews.

Her mastery extends beyond the kitchen, and Carrie has also been recognized for her partnership with local churches and community centers to feed and help seniors in the greater Fort Worth Community. Carrie Lou's is what it is today because of a beautiful lady named Carrie Lou Williams, Chef Carrie’s namesake and Great-Grandmother.

“Mrs. Carrie Lou Williams was a great inspiration to me. She always took great care of my brother and me so well. We always had three to four square meals a day plus snacks and lots of love. I sat and watched my mother and Great-Grandmother prepare meals from scratch and her famous peach cobbler that everyone loved. Learning and growing up in church and her home, I was bound to be great. The Lord has blessed my hands and given purpose to my gift of cooking. I serve by feeding people and help those unable to prepare a home-cooked meal for themselves. I am truly humbled by God’s blessings towards me.”

Carrie has been married to her best friend and love of her life for over 25 years. The couple has five children and eight grandchildren. Carrie Lou's Catering will always be available to serve you: "Large or Small, We Cater It All." Today, Chef Carrie carries forward that same spirit, blending tradition with her own creative flair to craft meals that nourish both body and soul. Her deep commitment to family is mirrored in her work, ensuring that every plate she serves reflects the love and gratitude that shaped her upbringing. Through her faith and culinary talents, Carrie continues to honor her family’s legacy, embracing each opportunity to serve others with kindness and a heartfelt touch.
